## 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how **The Mingler** (“the bot,” “we,” “us”) handles information when you use the bot on Discord.

The Mingler is a **third-party** tool. It is **not** operated by Toontown Rewritten or Disney. Using the bot does not change how TTR handles your in-game data—see [TTR’s Privacy Policy](https://www.toontownrewritten.com/privacy) for that.

We collect only what we need to run features you use. We **do not sell** your personal information.

---

## 2. Information we may collect

What we store depends on which features you and your server use. Categories include:

### Discord account information

- Discord user ID, username, and display name (as provided by Discord)
- Avatar URL and other profile fields Discord exposes to the bot
- Server (guild) IDs and channel IDs where you use bot commands, when needed for that feature

### Preferences you set

- Bot-wide settings such as default reply visibility (public/private), ping preferences, and DM preferences
- Per-server settings where applicable (for example, status or inventory preferences in servers that enable those features)

### Optional game-linked data (registration)

If you choose to use `/register` or related sync tools, we may store:

- Toon names, species, and other Companion API fields you sync
- Game progress snapshots (for example: gag tracks, tasks, suits, fishing, gardening, racing, or similar stats) **only as imported by your sync**
- Timestamps of last successful sync and basic sync status (success/error summaries)

Sync is **opt-in** and **user-initiated**. The optional push-sync script runs on **your** game PC, reads the local Companion API (`127.0.0.1` only), and sends data to the bot only when you run it with a token from your own `/register link`.

We do **not** collect your TTR password, payment information, or full Companion authorization secrets from your game session.

### Reminders, to-dos, and notifications

If you use reminder or to-do features, we store the text, schedules, and delivery preferences you provide so the bot can notify you.

### In-server game features

Some servers enable optional economy, shop, or similar bot features. If you use them, we may store balances, inventories, effects, or related gameplay state **for that bot feature only**.

### Support and feedback

If you submit `/tickets`, we store your message content, category, timestamps, and Discord identifiers needed to respond.

### Technical logs

We may keep limited operational logs (for example: command errors, sync failures, or moderation actions) to maintain and secure the bot. These logs are **not** published and are not used for advertising.

---

## 3. What we do not do

- We **do not sell** or rent your personal information.
- We **do not** use your messages, game data, or support content to **train generative AI models**.
- We **do not** expose one user’s registration or private command output to other users without a feature that explicitly requires it (and only within normal bot behavior, such as public leaderboards where configured).
- We **do not** log or publish one-time sync tokens, bot login secrets, or full raw Companion payloads in public channels.

---

## 4. How we use information

We use collected information to:

- Operate commands and features you request
- Link your Discord account to toon data **you** synced (for `/mytoons` and related tools)
- Send reminders, notifications, and support replies
- Prevent abuse, enforce blocks, and keep the bot reliable
- Improve features based on aggregated usage patterns (not by selling data)

## 5. How we share information

We share information only in these situations:

| Recipient | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| **Discord** | The bot runs on Discord’s platform; Discord processes interactions under [Discord’s Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy). |
| **Infrastructure providers** | Hosting, networking, or backup services that process data **on our behalf** to run the bot (under confidentiality and security obligations). |
| **Legal requirements** | If required by law, court order, or to protect users, the operator, or the public from harm. |

We do **not** share your synced toon data with other players except where a command’s normal output is intentionally public in your server (for example, a public leaderboard you opt into).

---

## 6. Data retention

We keep information **while it is needed** to provide features you use, unless you ask us to delete it or we no longer operate the bot.

Examples:

- Registration data: retained while you use linked features; removable on request
- Reminders and to-dos: retained until you delete them or they expire
- Support tickets: retained long enough to resolve issues and maintain a reasonable history
- Short-lived sync tokens: expire automatically; not kept as usable secrets after use

We may retain minimal backup copies for a limited period for disaster recovery, then delete them on a reasonable schedule.

---

## 7. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect stored data (access controls, hashed tokens where applicable, and avoiding public logging of secrets). No online service is perfectly secure; use optional features only if you trust the bot operator.

## 8. Your choices and rights

You can:

- **Not use** optional features such as `/register`
- Adjust **user settings** (`/usersettings`) where available
- **Request access, correction, or deletion** of bot-held data tied to your Discord account by contacting us via `/tickets` or **wazzuptoontown@gmail.com**
- **Remove the bot** from a server (server admins) or stop interacting with it

We will verify requests come from the Discord account in question (or an authorized representative) before fulfilling deletion or export requests.

**California residents:** You may have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA (know, delete, correct, opt out of sale). We do not sell personal information. Contact us at the email above to exercise rights.

**Other regions:** If local law grants you privacy rights, contact us and we will respond as required by applicable law.
